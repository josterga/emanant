Privacy Policy

Last updated May 1, 2026

Emanant is a tool for visualizing how far you can travel from where you stand. This policy explains what data we collect and how it's used.

What we collect

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used — events like rendering a walking circle, switching modes, or changing duration. These are logged without any personal identifier. Some events include coarse location context (neighborhood and city name, derived from your position at the time) to help us understand where the app is being used geographically. Your precise GPS coordinates are never included.

Google Analytics also automatically records your IP address and basic browser information as part of standard web analytics.

Map tiles. The map is rendered by Mapbox. Your browser makes requests to Mapbox's servers to load map imagery. Mapbox's own privacy policy governs that data.

What we don't collect

  • Precise location. Your GPS coordinates are used only on your device to draw the reach circle. They are never transmitted to us or stored anywhere.
  • Accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or user profile of any kind.
  • Personal identifiers. No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device IDs are collected.

Cookies

Google Analytics may set cookies in your browser to distinguish sessions. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Mapbox — map rendering and tile delivery

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
